Safe Home Solutions

is a web application that refers to a platform or website that connects individuals who need help with daily chores (such as cleaning , laundry and other household maintenance tasks) in collaboration with companies that help them provide that service. The portal serves as a marketplace for daily chores services , allowing individuals to easily find hire and play for the help they need.
Daily chores play important role in making daily life smooth and easy, if it is performed in proper way. Living without maids and helpers to do daily household chores and help them around with errands is among the biggest woes for senior citizen, working male and female. But it is hard to find an efficient, trained and trusted person who can execute all the things in proper way. Be it maid, cook, gardener, nanny, caretaker all plays a significant role for helping in domestic task. Manually it is also not possible to find a person in case of urgency. This portal is being developed to solve all these problems. Now this cumbersome task is just a piece of cake because the entire problem can be solved in just one click.

Proposed System:

The goal of a Safe Home Solutions is to make it easy and convenient for individuals to find reliable and affordable help with their daily chores, while also providing a platform for workers to find and secure work opportunities. This web application is being developed to show the details of all types of human resources that is providing by the company. User can search workers according to their needs. User will give feedback for employees as well as company.

Objective & Scope:

• Easy, secure and convenient way to find domestic workers on a single click.
• To provide experienced and trained workers.
• Flexible mode of hiring workers.
